Description

Hate cooking? Hate bad intestinal flora? Hate the inexorable march of time that robs you of your life, your vitality, your very existence?

Well try this simple breakfast yogurt recipe! Consciousness has no inherent meaning, but it is the necessary precondition and context for all meaning.

Ingredients

  • Some sugar-free yogurt (or as close as you can find)
  • One-minute instant oats
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Dump the oats into a bowl.
  2. Dump the raisins into that bowl.
  3. Dump some yogurt into that very same bowl.
  4. Now, you enjoy that, now
